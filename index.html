About

art + technology + business

I am an ex-software developer now aspiring graphic designer & digital artist. My goal as an artist & designer is to support communities and causes that I care about to uplift and elevate their voice in our digital landscape.

I graduated from WWU with a degree in Management Information Systems in 2014 and worked as a software developer specialized in data engineering until 2020. I wanted to take my technical & business skills and bring them into a more creative environment. I am returning to school and attending UW Bothell's Interactive Media Design program in fall of 2021.
